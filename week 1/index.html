Game Overview

Phasmophobia is a horor ghost hunting game where you and your friends investigate haunted places.Over here the player has the role to be a paranormal investigator.As a investigator you get several equipments to instigate the ghosts and summon them. There are proofs you need to collect while playing game. The ghost does hunts and as a player you need to hide and save your life. There are different places ,different types of ghosts every time. To win the game you need to identify the type of ghost and escape the place without dying.This game got recently famous on several streaming platforms.

Game Highlights

  • Enjoying with friends
  • Use cool ghost hunting equipments to hunt them
  • Different types of ghosts to discover
  • Creepy locations to explore and hide
